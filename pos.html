Gaming positives

Are you worried about gaming being bad for you or you child? do you think gaming affects scores at school?
These are some of the positives of gaming.

Gaming Improves Problem Solving.


Video games are all about completing that level or using tactics to beat you opponent, all of this involes problem solving, especially at higher levels.

Gaming improves your desicion making.


All through out games you are required to make split-second decision making, your choices can heavily affect you mission/objective and you learn pretty quickly which desisions are bad and which are good.
The more you game, the more you will have to make fast decisions, and you desicion will improve as you continue.

Improved goal setting abilities.


All games have an objective wheather it is to win a deathmatch or complete a specific task, all these things require you to break down the goals and work towards solving them bit by bit.
To play games at high levels you have to have fine goal setting skills.
